SRv6

Have you heard about SRv6? It is a technology based on IPv6 that enables network routing in a more flexible, scalable, and programmable way. I find it very interesting because it not only helps direct traffic more efficiently, but it can also simplify the operation of modern networks and improve traffic engineering. Even so, I would like to learn more about its use cases, its advantages over other technologies, and the impact it could have on the future of telecommunications and network infrastructures.
